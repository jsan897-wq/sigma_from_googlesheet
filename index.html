Analyst Guide

The standard deviation model maps the current asset price against calculated Sigma bands. This acts as statistical boundaries to spot overextended market conditions.

  • SIGMA +3 / +2: Standard resistance levels. Prices reaching here are statistically overextended upward.
  • CLOSE: Point of equilibrium.
  • SIGMA -3 / -2: Standard support levels. Overextended downward.
